How to get involved

We welcome any organization or individual interested in adopting the IIIF, developing software to support it, or giving feedback on the effort to join our discussion list at: 
https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/iiif-discuss
There is also a lower traffic announcements list at: 
https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/iiif-announce
The IIIF community GitHub page is at: 
http://github.com/iiif
Join the conversation on the IIIF Slack team: 
http://bit.ly/iiif-slack
There are also bi-weekly teleconferences, the details of which are posted in the iiif-discuss list. All community members are welcome and encouraged to participate in the calls. Notes from the calls are posted in this Google Drive folder.

Community groups

There are a number of community interest and working groups that focus on different areas. IIIF community groups are open to participation from all interested parties.
All participants in all IIIF activities are expected to follow the IIIF code of conduct.
