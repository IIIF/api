Community Driven

The IIIF is driven by a group of research libraries, national libraries, and nonprofit image repositories committed to opening access to cherished image resources.

Defined APIs

The IIIF is anchored by two well defined application programming interfaces developed and vetted by an open and rigorous community process.

Plug ’n’ Play Software

The IIIF encourages and supports the development of compatible image serving and viewing software that is easy to install and provides a dazzling user experience.
