How to get involved

We welcome any organization or individual interested in adopting the IIIF, developing software to support it, or giving feedback on the effort to join our discussion list at: 
https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/iiif-discuss
There is also a lower traffic announcements list at: 
https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/iiif-announce
There are also bi-weekly teleconferences, the details of which are posted in the iiif-discuss list. All community members are welcome and encouraged to participate in the calls. Notes from the calls are posted in the site news.
The IIIF community GitHub page is at: 
http://github.com/iiif
